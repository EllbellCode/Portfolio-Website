Publications

Towards Mechanised Consensus in Isabelle

In this paper, I build on the framework I developed in my master's thesis and formally verify Proof-of-Work consensus. I do this by modelling a blockchain as a binary tree and specify consensus using the specification outlined in the Bitcoin Backbone Protocol paper. This paper was accepted by FMBC and I presented it in Luxembourg!

A Framework for Verifying Blockchain Consensus in Isabelle

My Master's Thesis! In this paper, I create a framework for verifying blockchain consensus protocols in Isabelle. In this framework, I model blockchain as a binary tree and create several key functions and lemmas that lay the foundations for verifying Proof-of-Work consensus. This paper received a distinction.
